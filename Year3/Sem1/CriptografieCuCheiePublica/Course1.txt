Cryptosystem: 5-tuple(P,C,K,E,D)
	P->set of possible paintext characters/blocks(non-cripted text)
	C->set of possible ciphertext characters/blocks(cripted text)
	K->set of possible keys(most of the time numbers , may be functions etc)

	eK:P->C ; dK:C->P ; 
	dK(eK(x)):x (if you encript and the descript a message you should get the initial text)
